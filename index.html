About the project:

This data visualization shows the total number of rides taken on Zagster bikes in Bend OR since it’s introduction in September 2016. The data is colleected through October of 2018. By referencing a database full of JSON data hosted through “heropuapp.com” containing all of the collected Zagster data. Using the Chart.js library, you can see the amount of rides that were taken per year, per month, and per hour. With this data expressed in bar graphs you can compare the popularity from year to year, month to month, and hour to hour. Also with the data the popularity from season tto season can also be seen.
